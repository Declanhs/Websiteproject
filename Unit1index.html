- 1.1 Describing the purpose of IT in modern business.

The main purpose of IT in modern business is convenience, a computer program will reduce the number of mistakes that would traditionally be made without IT. IT software and hardware don’t have the same limitations as humans. IT works consistently without becoming ‘tired’, something you’d see if someone did the work manually. Modern IT is used in every sector of business in every level, these areas include communication, organisation, productivity or research. All these areas of business are highly dependent on modern IT. Some other key roles of IT are data management systems, customer relationship management, inventory management systems and relationships between customers, employees and employers. [1]

1.4 Describe the risks that might impact digital projects – what causes digital projects to fail and why? [3]

There are many risks for digital projects, and I am going to describe the ones I consider to be why projects fail. Teams focusing too much on short term goals, this can make the overall project be neglected. When it comes to putting everything together, there wasn’t enough time spent on the long-term priorities due to all the time wasted on short term. Another reason companies’ digital projects fail is due to poor planning, this could be poor planning or just a poorly executed plan, but both have the same outcome, failure. Trying to do too much at one time can also damage your overall project, you must always be mindful of overloading your team. This is because a quality product takes time to produce perfectly if you rush it you will have a rushed produced.
